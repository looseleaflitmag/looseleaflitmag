LOOSE LEAF LIT MAG

Loose Leaf's mission is to design and distribute a free student run literary magazine to cultivate an inclusive art space for young creatives in New Brunswick.
We distribute our magazine around the campuses about once or twice a semester. We accept submissions of art, photography, and writing and publish it in our magazine.
